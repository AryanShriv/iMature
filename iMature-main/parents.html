THE MOST IMPORTANT SUBJECT

Is your child suffering from the same addiction

Is your child spending excessive time playing online games? This can lead to addiction and affect their physical health, academic performance, and social skills.

Do you notice your child constantly checking their social media accounts? This can indicate a social media addiction that can have negative impacts on mental health and relationships.

Is your child spending a lot of time watching videos on YouTube or other streaming platforms? This can lead to a video streaming addiction, which can also affect their productivity and relationships.

Does your child have trouble disconnecting from their devices and feel anxious when they are not using them? This can be a sign of technology addiction, which can have serious consequences on their mental and physical health.

Is your child engaging in online gambling activities? This can lead to a gambling addiction, which can have severe financial, legal, and psychological consequences.

Does your child display a compulsive need to shop online or spend money on in-game purchases? This can be a sign of online shopping addiction, which can have a negative impact on their financial wellbeing and mental health.

How You Can Be A Hero For Your Child Through DCIM?

Parents can become a hero for their child by familiarizing themselves with DCIM and its core concepts, including digital literacy, responsible behavior online, and online safety.

Parents can set an example for their children by modeling responsible behavior online, including respecting the rights of others, protecting personal information, and avoiding harmful online behaviors.

Teach children about online safety: Parents can educate their children about online safety, including how to identify and avoid online predators, scams, and other digital risks.

Parents can teach their children about responsible social media use, including avoiding cyberbullying, respecting the privacy of others, and understanding the consequences of online actions.
